# Pricing And Packaging (Phase 4 Draft)

## Plans

### Free
- Single user, local only
- `init`, `lock`, `doctor`, `policy`, `secret-lint`, `activate`, `roi`

### Team
- Suggested price: `$12-$18 / developer / month`
- Team registry sharing (`push`, `pull`, `registry-ls`)
- Audit events
- Token-protected registry endpoints

### Business
- Suggested price: `$29+ / developer / month`
- SSO-integrated deployment pattern (token/JWT + proxy)
- Policy packs and stricter governance defaults
- Extended retention for audit events

### Enterprise
- Annual contract
- Private control plane, custom support, SLA

## ROI Inputs
Use `devsync roi` with:
- team size
- monthly hires
- onboarding hours before/after
- drift incidents per developer
- engineering hourly rate
- price per developer

## Early Commercial Motion
- Paid pilot package target: `$1k-$3k / month`
- Convert successful pilots to annual team/business contracts
